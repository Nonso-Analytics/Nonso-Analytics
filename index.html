About

Who I Am

Hi, I'm Chinonso Okonkwo. I'm a Machine Learning Engineer and Data Analyst who enjoys using technology to solve practical problems. I've always been curious about how things work, and that curiosity naturally pulled me into data and AI. What motivates me is the idea of building tools that feel simple, helpful, and accessible, especially in communities where technology can create real opportunities.

Most of my work sits at the intersection of machine learning and real-world impact. I've built systems like a voice-driven financial assistant that helps unbanked users access services in local languages, medical image classifiers that support early diagnosis, and models that analyze patterns in customer behavior or financial risk. I enjoy working through the entire process, from shaping the problem to building the model to making sure the final solution feels understandable and usable.

Beyond the technical side, I care a lot about clarity, communication, and continuous improvement. Whether I'm designing a dashboard, cleaning messy data, or experimenting with deep learning architectures, I try to keep things purposeful and grounded. Each project teaches me something new about people, data, and design, and I'm always excited to keep growing and creating work that actually makes a difference.

When I'm not coding or analyzing data, you'll find me creating educational content on Youtube, learning a new language, or practicing chess on Duolingo.
